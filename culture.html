Heaven

Hebrews 13:14 " For this world is not our permanent home; we are looking forward to a home yet to come."

I Come from Gods Heavenly Kingdom because I am His Daughter. He says in "If you were of the world, the world would love you as its own; but because you are not of the world, but I chose you out of the world, therefore the world hates you." He chose me out of this world to be one of His own. When he died for my sins on that Cross and rose again, he went home to His Father in heaven. Tonga

OTUA MO TONGA KO HOKU TOFIA

Tonga is a very small island located in the South pacific Ocean and is one of the polynesian pacific islands along with others such as samoa, Cook Island, Fiji.. My parents are both Tongan. My Dad lived in kolomotu'a Tonga tapu and my Mum lived in Haveluloto Tonga Tapu. I also lived in haveluloto with my Mum before moving to Nz. As a Tongan we love our food and my favourite is Faikakai ngou'a and otai.
