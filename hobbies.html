Sports (Netball)

Netball is a ball sport played by two teams of seven players. Games are played on a rectangular court with raised goal rings at each end. The object is to score goals from within a defined area, by throwing a ball into a ring attached to a 3.05 metres (10 feet) high post.

I play in the senior netball team for my school as Goal keeper (GK-Defender). I love the game of netball because i like jumping and contesting for the ball. Netball, is a non-contact sport which has rules and consequences for contacts that happens in a game. It can really trains the mind and body to be well diciplined with contacts.
